Frequently asked
questions

How does Roazr track WhatsApp conversions?

Roazr integrates with the WhatsApp Business API to match incoming conversations to ad click IDs. Every message, qualified lead, and closed deal is mapped back to the exact campaign, ad set, and creative.

Do I need a developer to set it up?

No. Setup takes under 10 minutes. Connect your ad accounts, add a tracking pixel, and link your WhatsApp Business — all through the dashboard with no code required.

Which ad platforms are supported?

Roazr supports Meta (Facebook & Instagram), Google Ads, and TikTok Ads. We're adding more platforms regularly based on customer demand.

Can Roazr track offline payments?

Yes. Bank transfers, POS payments, and mobile money transactions are automatically matched to the originating ad through our AI revenue parsing engine.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Join the waitlist and we'll set you up with a 14-day free trial during onboarding. No credit card required.

What markets does Roazr support?

Roazr is built for WhatsApp-driven markets. We currently support Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa, Tanzania, and are expanding across Africa and emerging markets.

How is pricing structured?

Simple monthly plans with no hidden fees. Nigerian businesses pay ₦49,000/mo, international teams pay $89/mo. Pay only for what you use.
